VR Wheelchair Soccer

An Unreal Engine VR game with AI-driven opponents for Oculus Quest 2, using Blueprints and C++.

← Back to Home

Overview

Developed a full VR soccer experience featuring adaptive AI players, physics-based interactions, and intuitive controls optimized for VR immersion.

Key Features

  • Dynamic AI behavior with behavior trees
  • Hand-tracked ball control and physics
  • Optimized for Oculus Quest 2 performance

Game Basics

  • Hook up your Oculus VR headset to a PC and launch the game
  • Choose between a tutorial or singleplayer match against AI
  • Navigate in your wheelchair using gestures for braking, turning, and moving
  • Knock around the ball to score in the net
  • Stay seated—tipping over is possible due to realistic physics

Technologies Used

  • Unreal Engine 4 – Blueprints & C++ for game mechanics
  • Oculus Quest 2 – headset for development and testing

Future Plans

  • Add multiplayer matches against other players online
  • Revamp the user interface for greater intuitiveness
  • Expand with additional maps and game modes
