Overview

Welcome to the 3D Racing Game! Created for Game Design 1 at University of Michigan-Dearborn, this Unity-powered experience features realistic car physics, boost mechanics, and competitive AI races—complete a lap to claim victory.

Features

  • Realistic car physics with drifts around turns
  • Boost function for extra bursts of speed
  • Challenging AI opponent for competitive races
  • Beautiful 3D graphics and immersive sound effects

How to Play

  • Download and run the game on your PC
  • Use arrow keys or WASD to control your car
  • Hold Shift to drift and Space to boost
  • Stay on track, avoid obstacles, and finish one lap first to win
